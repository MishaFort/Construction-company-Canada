Track & Structure Monitoring

At Phoenix Rail Engineering services, we have the capability to undertake various methods of monitoring from a simple low risk TMP to High Risk TMP.

We have the ability to either undertake remote monitoring where access is limited to conventional track monitoring.

All TMPs written & risk assessed in line with NR standard NR/L2/Civ/177.

We also undertake all types of Bridge & Structure monitoring.

Site Surveys

At Phoenix Rail Engineering services, we have the capability to undertake all surveying requirements, from topographical surveys, design validation surveys.

We also undertake surveys for rail replacement works including IBJ Recoveries & installation with a provision of a detailed siting form.

CRE/CRT Management, TSR/ESR & THB

At Phoenix Rail Engineering Services, we have very experienced CRE (C) Track capability that can support your projects, along with this we offer CRT Management that also have the associated capability to erect & remove TSRs & ESRs.

We can also supply related TSR/ESR Designs.

All designs are reviewed by our CRE/Track Hand-backs prior to installation.

Tamping & Stressing

With engineering at the heart of everything we do at Phoenix Rail Engineering Services, we have the capability to undertake all Tamping & Stressing requirements.

Our in house engineering team can ensure all Tamping & Stressing required will be completed to the relevant NR Standards for Tamping & Stressing.

All H&S AMP Documentation also provided along with all As built information.

Construction & Engineering management support

Phoenix Rail Engineering Services, have the capabilities to support all of your engineering & management requirements, whether supplying a Track CRE to support your projects to on site & office support.

With a team of industry accredited Engineers & Construction Managers, we’re there to support.
